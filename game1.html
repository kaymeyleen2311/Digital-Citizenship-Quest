🎲 How to Play

  • • Roll the dice to move around the board
  • • Answer questions correctly to earn points
  • • First player to reach space 20 with correct answer wins!
  • • Learn about digital citizenship along the way

Scoring System

  • • Correct Answer: +5 points
  • • Wrong Answer: Lose 3 points + Chaos increases
  • • Complete bonus challenges: +3 points
  • • 5 chaos meter means eliminated

📚 Question Categories

  • • 🪑 Posture & Ergonomics
  • • ⏰ Screen Time Management
  • • 🛡️ Internet Safety & Security
  • • 🖥️ Computer Hardware and Functions
  • • ⚙️ Basic Computer Operations

👥 Multiplayer Rules

  • • 1-5 players can join the game
  • • Take turns rolling the dice
  • • Help each other learn together
  • • Celebrate everyone's progress!
